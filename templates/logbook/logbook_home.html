How to use ML(fieldbook)

Creating and Setting Account

01

To start using ML(fieldbook), simply sign up by providing your Full Name, Practical Training location, and other required information. Once registered, you can easily log in using your chosen username and password.

Account Details

Accessing Aida From Projects

02

Once you log in to ML(fieldbook), you will be directed to the website main page. From there, click on the "ML(fieldbook)" tab and select "Explore" to access the app. Feel free to explore and navigate through various projects

Home Screen ML(fieldbook) navigation

Creating Logbook

03

After logging in, you can find the navigation menu at the top right corner. Click on the "Catalog" link to explore the available options. Alternatively, you can open "My Logbooks" to view a list of logbooks you have created. From this page, you have the option to create a new logbook and start organizing your training data efficiently.

Initializing Logbook

04

Once you click on the + Create logbook button, a simple form will appear, prompting you to enter the week number and the starting date (Monday's date) for the week. This information will help organize your logbook effectively, allowing you to keep track of your practical training progress systematically.

Creating Entries & operations

05

Upon creating your logbook, you'll find two helpful buttons to guide you through your practical training journey. The first button will allow you to generate all five entries for the week automatically, providing you with a convenient starting point that you can later edit and customize as needed. The second button will lead you to the operations page, where you can create and update your weekly operations

Update Your Operation Diagram

06

Lastly and Important. If you have a diagram created already, just upload it by taping/clicking on a diagram box. The appearance of the diagram in the preview will be the same as in the document generated.
